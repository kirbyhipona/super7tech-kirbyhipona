Question 4 : Answer - 4
The circle alternates between white and black as it shifts to the right in each column, while moving downward in each row. Conversely, the square changes its color between black and white, shifting to the left within each column and ascending in every row. Simultaneously, the horizontal line progresses upwards in each column.
